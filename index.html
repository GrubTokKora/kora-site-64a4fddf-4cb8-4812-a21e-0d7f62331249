Our Story: Heart Movement

Welcome to Jaiwai Thai Kitchen, a family-owned and operated restaurant established in 2015 in Georgetown, Texas. Our name, Jaiwai, means 'heart movement' in Thai, symbolizing our passion for sharing soulful, homemade Thai dishes. We bring regional flavors from northern Thailand, including our signature Chicken Khao Soi. We believe food connects people, and every dish is prepared with care, tradition, and love, just like for family.
