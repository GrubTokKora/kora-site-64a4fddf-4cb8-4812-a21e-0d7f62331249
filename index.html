Our story

Heart movement,
turned up to Thai hot.

Welcome to Jaiwai Thai Kitchen — a family-owned restaurant established in 2015 in Georgetown, Texas. Our name, Jaiwai, means "heart movement" in Thai, symbolizing our passion for sharing soulful, homemade Thai dishes with the community we love.

We bring regional flavours from northern Thailand, including our signature Chicken Khao Soi. Every dish is prepared with care, tradition and love — just like for family. No white tablecloths, no fuss — just big flavour, generous plates and a room that feels like home.
